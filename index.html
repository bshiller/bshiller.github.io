About me

My research focuses on industrial organization and technological change. More specifically, my research investigates the impacts of consumer data collection (pricing strategies, market concentration), consumer countermeasures (ad blocking), distribution formats (digital distribution, pricing, and resale restrictions), and information disclosure / mechanism design (vehicle crashworthiness ratings).
